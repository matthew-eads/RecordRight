The days field specifies which days to send the reminder; '1' will send it every day, '2' will be every other day, and so on.

The hours fields specify at what time of day to run the reminder. Every '1' hour will send it each hour, '2' hours will send it every other hour, etc. The between <start hour> and <end hour> fields are optional, but if specified will constrain when messages are sent. For example 'every 2 hours from 6 to 18' will send a message every other hour, starting at 6am, ending at 6pm.

So, to send a message at 6am and 6pm every other day, you would specify 'every 2 days, every 12 hours, between 6 and 18'.

Lastly, you must specify when to stop sending the reminder - you can specify a specific date to stop, or the total number of messages to send. You can specify both, and the messages will stop based on whichever happens first.
